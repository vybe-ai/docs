Customers can cancel their ongoing VYBE subscriptions on their account page on the [VYBE website.](https://vybe.im/settings?tab=billing)

### Frequently asked questions
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="What happens when I cancel my subscription?">
    When you cancel your subscription, your subscription will still be active until the end of the current billing period. 
    After the current billing period, you will no longer be charged for VYBE.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
