## Why Choose Nutshell?

In today's fast-paced world, every word counts. Nutshell is built for busy professionals who need:

- **Real-time transcription & batch processing:** Capture live discussions or review recordings with ease.
- **Maximum privacy:** All processing happens locally—your data stays on your device.
- **Interactive insights:** Ask questions during or after your meetings and get instant, context-aware responses.
- **Customizable assistance:** Use tailored prompts to get the information you need, exactly how you want it.
- **Offline functionality:** Stay productive even without an internet connection.

## Responsible and Ethical Use

Nutshell includes an in-app alert when live transcription starts, reminding you to inform all participants. Always ensure compliance with local laws and secure proper consent before recording.
