## System requirements
Before installing Nutshell, please ensure your device meets the following requirements:

1. **Device:** A MacBook with an M1 (or later) Apple Silicon chip.
1. **Operating system:** MacOS 13 or later.

## Download Nutshell
To download Nutshell:

1. Visit [withnutshell.com](https://withnutshell.com/)
2. Click the "Download" button at the top of the page
3. Once downloaded, open the installer and follow the installation prompts

### Frequently asked questions
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="I have a MacBook with an Intel chip, can I still use Nutshell?">
      We recommend using a MacBook with an Apple Silicon chip. Since Nutshell processes AI models locally to ensure privacy, 
      Intel-based MacBooks may encounter performance issues, leading to a less-than-optimal experience.
