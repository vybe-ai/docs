## Start to finish: Transcription with Nutshell

For this example, we'll be using the live transcription settings (default) and demonstrating with Zoom. The process is the same for other meeting platforms.

<Accordion title='1. Ensure "Nutshell Speaker" is selected'>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Nutshell desktop app">
    
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open system settings">
    Navigate to your system's sound settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select output device">
    Choose "Nutshell Speaker" as your output device (This is created when Nutshell is opened, if you don't see it, try closing and reopening Nutshell desktop app)
  </Step>
</Steps>

    <Frame>
        <video
            src="/images/nutshell_speaker_at_os.mp4"
            autoPlay
            loop
            controls
            playsInline
        />
    </Frame>

  <Frame caption="Indictor to show Nutshell Speaker is properly selected in your system settings">
    <img src="/images/nutshell_speaker_selected.png" alt="Selecting Nutshell Speaker in system settings" />
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. Configure Your Audio Devices">
  <Info>
    Nutshell will use your system's default microphone. You can change this in your system settings if needed.
  </Info>

At the top of the Nutshell app, use the dropdown menu to select which speaker device you want to use. If you're using headphones, connect them now.

  <Frame caption="Select the speaker device you want to hear output from">
    <img src="/images/nutshell_speaker_selected.png" alt="Selecting Nutshell Speaker in system settings" />
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. Start Your First Recording">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Click New Conversation">
      Look for the "New Conversation" button in the top left corner of the app
      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/new_conversation_button.png" alt="New Conversation button" />
      </Frame>
    </Step>
    
    <Step title="Start Recording">
      Click the "Start Recording" button to begin transcription
      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/start_recording.png" alt="Start Recording button" />
      </Frame>
    </Step>
  </Steps>

  <Tip>
    You can start recording even after your meeting has begun. The steps shown here demonstrate starting Nutshell before joining a meeting.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. Join Your Meeting">
  <Steps>
    <Step title="Join Your Zoom Meeting (works with other platforms too)">
      When joining Zoom, ensure "Nutshell Speaker" is selected as your audio output device. If Nutshell Speaker is set as your system's output device, Zoom will automatically use it.

      For microphone, you can select whichever microphone you want to use.
    <Frame>
        <video 
            src="/images/zoom_example.mp4"
            autoPlay
            loop
            controls
            playsInline
        />
    </Frame>
    </Step>
  </Steps>

  <Note>
    The same process applies to other meeting platforms. Always ensure "Nutshell Speaker" is selected as the audio output device.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="5. Stopping Your Transcription Session">
  ### Stopping the Transcription Session

Click the pause button in the top right corner to stop the transcription session.

{" "}
<Frame>
  <img
    src="/images/stop_recording.png"
    alt="Stop recording button"
  />
</Frame>

### Reviewing and Editing

After stopping the recording, you can:

- Play back the recorded audio
- Review the transcription
- Re-run transcription if needed for better accuracy

  <Tip>
    You can re-run transcription multiple times on the same recording to improve accuracy or correct any issues.
  </Tip>
</Accordion>
